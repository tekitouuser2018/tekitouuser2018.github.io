TomaToma Fan Creation Guidelines

These Guidelines set out the rules for fan creation activities related to TomaToma, so that fans can enjoy creating and sharing their works safely and respectfully.

You may engage in fan creation activities within the scope of these Guidelines without obtaining individual permission. However, these Guidelines do not waive any copyrights, trademark rights, or other rights related to TomaToma or its related content.

1. Basic Policy

We welcome creative activities by fans.

However, in order to protect the character's image, world, third-party rights, and social safety, any use that violates these Guidelines is prohibited.

These Guidelines do not waive any copyrights or other rights. All copyrights, trademark rights, and other rights related to TomaToma, including illustrations, settings, names, logos, and other related content, belong to us or to the rightful rights holders.

2. Permitted Uses

As a general rule, fan creations by individuals for non-commercial or hobby purposes are permitted.

Examples of permitted uses

  • Creating and posting fan art on social media
  • Creating and publishing comics, illustrations, novels, videos, cosplay, and similar works
  • Small-scale distribution of doujinshi, acrylic keychains, stickers, and similar items
  • Introductions, impressions, and reviews on personal websites, social media, and video platforms
  • Parodies, arrangements, and fan works that add new creative expression to the original work

However, simply copying, tracing, cropping, or lightly modifying official materials may be regarded as unauthorized use of official materials rather than a fan creation.

3. Non-commercial and Hobby Use

The following types of use are generally considered non-commercial or hobby use.

  • Small-scale distribution at doujin or fan events
  • Sales intended only to recover actual costs, such as production, printing, materials, and shipping costs
  • Publication on personal social media accounts or video platforms
  • Exhibition or distribution as part of ordinary fan activities

Uses requiring prior permission

  • Use by corporations, organizations, or businesses
  • Continuous or large-scale sales
  • Use primarily for made-to-order production, mass production, wholesale, or consignment sales
  • Use of the character for advertising, promotion, campaigns, or tie-ins
  • Use in apps, games, NFTs, AI training datasets, generative AI services, or commercial asset collections
  • Any other use that we determine to be highly commercial in nature

4. Prohibited Uses

4.1 Use that may be mistaken for official content

  • Displaying the work in a way that may imply it is official, authorized, affiliated, sponsored, or endorsed
  • Unauthorized use of official logos, official marks, or designs resembling an official website
  • Using names, icons, or profiles that may be mistaken for official accounts
  • Presenting the work as if it were published or announced by us

When publishing fan creations, please include a notice such as the following where appropriate.

This is a fan-made work and is not an official work.

4.2 Use without sufficient originality

  • Reposting official illustrations, images, videos, audio, or text as-is
  • Using official materials only by tracing, cropping, changing colors, or making minor edits
  • Creating summary sites, asset distribution pages, or reposting services whose main purpose is to view, store, or redistribute official content
  • Turning official materials into merchandise for sale

4.3 Use that seriously harms the character's image

  • Expressions that seriously harm the credibility or image of the character, the work, or us
  • Malicious modifications, insults, or use primarily intended to provoke controversy
  • Antisocial, violent, discriminatory, threatening, or harassing expressions
  • Use involving defamation, slander, invasion of privacy, or other harm to third parties
  • Use that violates laws, regulations, or public order and morals

4.4 Use for political, religious, or ideological activities

The following uses are prohibited.

  • Use for the purpose of supporting, criticizing, or promoting a specific political party, politician, candidate, political organization, or political movement
  • Use for election activities, political campaigns, or the spread of political claims
  • Use for the purpose of promoting a specific religion, religious organization, religious activity, missionary activity, or religious solicitation
  • Use for the purpose of expressing support for or opposition to a specific ideology, belief, social movement, or organization
  • Use that makes it appear as though the character holds a specific political or religious position

Prohibited: TomaToma may not be used as a symbol, spokesperson, advertising material, or promotional material for political or religious claims.

4.5 Restrictions on users and accounts

We may exclude the following individuals, organizations, or accounts from the permission granted under these Guidelines at our discretion.

  • Persons who have been finally convicted of serious crimes
  • Antisocial forces or persons engaged in similar activities
  • Persons who promote illegal acts, violence, fraud, exploitation, discrimination, hate, or extremist conduct
  • Individuals, organizations, or accounts whose primary purpose is the daily promotion, solicitation, or incitement of political, religious, ideological, or social movement activities
  • Persons whom we determine may harm our credibility, the character's world, or brand image

This restriction is not based on any particular ideology, belief, or political position itself. It is intended to prevent the character from being used for, or being mistaken as associated with, political, religious, or antisocial activities.

5. Adult-oriented Expressions

For adult-oriented, highly sexual, excessively violent, or grotesque expressions, you must comply with publication venue rules, age restrictions, zoning requirements, and the terms of each platform.

However, the following expressions are prohibited.

  • Sexual expressions involving characters who may be perceived as minors
  • Expressions that affirm or promote sexual exploitation, coercion, violence, or crime
  • Expressions that seriously harm the character's dignity or image
  • Adult-oriented expressions published without sufficient access restrictions or zoning

If we determine that a work is inappropriate, we may request suspension, removal, or correction of the work.

6. AI and Generative AI

The following uses are prohibited.

  • Using official illustrations, fan creations, or character images as AI training data without permission
  • Creating, distributing, or selling AI models, LoRA models, prompt collections, asset collections, or similar materials that reproduce the character
  • Publishing AI-generated content in a way that may be mistaken for official content
  • Using AI-generated content in a way that may infringe our rights or third-party rights

If you publish AI-generated content, please clearly indicate that AI was used and take sufficient care to avoid any confusion with official works.

7. Third-party Rights

If your fan creation uses third-party copyrighted works, trademarks, people, characters, music, photographs, videos, logos, or other materials, please check the relevant rights holders' permissions and guidelines.

We are not responsible for any disputes or problems arising from combining TomaToma with third-party rights or materials.

8. Credit Notice

When publishing or distributing fan creations, please include the following notice where reasonably possible.

Original character: TomaToma
This is a fan-made work and is not an official work.
© 2026 TomaToma. All Rights Reserved.

If you would like to make the rights holder or brand name clearer, you may also use the following notice.

© 2026 TomaToma / Mackerelish. All Rights Reserved.

9. Inquiries and Individual Permission

Please contact us in advance if you wish to engage in any of the following uses.

  • Commercial use
  • Use by corporations or organizations
  • Use for advertising, promotion, or tie-ins
  • Mass production or large-scale sales
  • Use in apps, games, video works, publications, or similar media
  • Any use where you are unsure whether permission is required

We may not be able to respond to every inquiry. If we do not respond individually, that does not mean the relevant use is permitted under these Guidelines.

Contact: Contact Form

10. Requests for Suspension or Removal

If we determine that a fan creation is inappropriate, we may request suspension of publication, suspension of sales, removal, or correction of the fan creation, with or without disclosing the reason.

If we make such a request, the user must promptly comply with it.

11. Changes to These Guidelines

We may change these Guidelines without prior notice.

The revised Guidelines will take effect when published. Please always check the latest version before engaging in fan creation activities.

12. Disclaimer

Fan creation activities are carried out at the user's own responsibility.

We are not responsible for any disputes, damages, or problems that arise between users and third parties in connection with fan creation activities.

For the sale of fan-made goods, please refer to the Fan-made Goods License Terms.
