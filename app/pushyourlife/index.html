Features

A TODO app focused on doing, not managing.

Keep the structure simple. Split big tasks into child tasks, sort them by difficulty, and archive what you no longer need in the main queue.

🎯

Difficulty tabs

Organize tasks into Easy, Middle, and Great so you can choose the right task for your current energy.

🌿

Parent-child tasks

Break large tasks into smaller child tasks without turning your task list into a complex project management tool.

📦

Archive and restore

Move completed or paused tasks out of the way, then restore them later when you need them again.
