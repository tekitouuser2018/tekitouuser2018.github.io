2. Information You Enter Into the App

2.1. Task data

The App allows you to create and manage task-related information, which may include:

  • task titles;
  • task memos;
  • task icons or emojis;
  • task difficulty settings such as Easy, Middle, or Great;
  • due dates;
  • completion status;
  • archive status;
  • parent and child task relationships;
  • task ordering or position; and
  • other information you choose to enter into the App.

2.2. Local storage

Task data is generally stored locally on your device using local app storage, such as an on-device database. We do not operate a user account system or cloud synchronization service for this App at the time this Privacy Policy is published.

Because task data is stored locally, we generally cannot view, access, restore, or recover your task data. Your task data may be lost if, for example, you delete the App, reset the local database, clear app data, reinstall the App, change devices, lose your device, or experience a device or software failure.

2.3. Sensitive information

The App is a general-purpose task management tool and is not intended for storing highly sensitive information. You should avoid entering information such as passwords, government identification numbers, financial account details, medical records, private legal documents, or other information that requires special protection.
